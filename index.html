Who We Are

We are Innovative Point, a tech and solution agency with partners worldwide. We design thoughtful digital solutions to all types of problems.

Our approach to an I.T. partnership is bold. From the very first interaction, and every step along the way, we're committed to doing what's right for your business, your productivity, and your bottom line. From the questions we ask, to the strategies we formulate, and the solutions we put in place, we're in this with you, to collaborate and innovate on technology that drives your business forward. Our client base spans from new start-ups to small enterprise organizations and everything in between. We leverage technology to move people and businesses forward. You have unique business needs that don't match some out-of-the-box, cookie-cutter solution. We'll custom create software solutions that let you run your business your way.

Strategy

We like to take a step back to ask questions and get to the root of the matter. Together, we can then create a strategy that aligns your business drivers, cultural impact, and the value of IT in your organization.

Analysis

With the right strategy in place, we develop your requirements. Taking a 10,000-foot view of your goals and resources, we’ll determine the best course of action and be honest about what needs to happen.

Implementation

We don’t play favorites; we’re hardware and software agnostic. Build it? Buy it? Whatever’s right for your business is what’s right for us.

Support

We’ll stand behind you until the solution is up and running, beautifully. It’s not about holding you hostage to our knowledge; it’s about empowering you with it so you can stay on the front end of technology right alongside us.
